Types of Accounts

Fan

Fans are the default account. Fans are able to post and delete pictures. Fans love the game and want to share their team's success. Sign up? You're a fan!

Coach

Coaches are reporters connected to a team. They can report scores, add game notes, post and delete pictures in addition to managing their team page Coaches also have special access to our Admin team for any problems. While we would prefer that the actual coach keep the "Coach" account, we welcome responsible student managers. If you believe you should have a coach account, please click the "CONTACT" button below

Reporter

Reporters are fans of the public league. They are reliable reporters, allowed to report scores, add game notes, and post pictures.
